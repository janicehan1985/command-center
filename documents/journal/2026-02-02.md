## YouTube Download Research

**Methods that work:**
1. `--cookies-from-browser chrome` - Most reliable, extracts cookies directly
2. Export cookies: `yt-dlp --cookies-from-browser chrome --cookies cookies.txt`
3. Fix signature errors with `--user-agent`, `--extractor-args`, `--sleep-interval`

**Alternative tools:**
- Stacher (GUI for yt-dlp)
- NewPipe (Android)
- SaveFrom.net (Web)
- 4K Video Downloader

**Key command patterns:**
```bash
yt-dlp --cookies-from-browser chrome "URL"
yt-dlp --cookies-from-browser chrome --user-agent "Mozilla/5.0..." --sleep-interval 3 "URL"
```
